Anyone can be a philanthropist.

In our early days, we seeded teams on both coasts with cash, and asked them to go forth and do as much good as possible. Given full creative freedom and ongoing support, they gave powerfully and passionately to bring their unique visions to life. Realizing we were on to something big, we set out to create a more sustainable, scalable model and spread the joy of giving every day.
